About Elpis

Elpis means Hope. Founded in 2026 by Kelvin Njoroge, Maxwell Basweti, and Dennis Guchu, we bring hope, dignity, and professional healthcare directly to your home. Our caregivers are trained to deliver personalized and respectful care that enhances comfort and quality of life.

Mission: To provide compassionate, professional, and reliable home-based healthcare services that improve quality of life for our clients and support their families.

Vision: To be the leading trusted home healthcare provider across Kenya, setting the standard for excellence and care.

Our Approach: Experienced caregivers, culturally sensitive service, and modern healthcare standards ensure comfort, safety, and dignity for every client.

Service Areas: Nairobi County, Kiambu County, Machakos, Kajiado, and surrounding regions.

Importance of Elderly Care at Home

Providing care in the comfort of the home allows seniors to maintain independence, feel safer, and receive personalized attention from trained caregivers. Home care also supports families, reducing stress and ensuring professional supervision of daily activities, hygiene, and emotional support.

Managing Medication Safely

Proper medication management at home is critical for health and recovery. Our caregivers ensure medications are administered on time, track dosages, monitor reactions, and communicate with healthcare providers to prevent errors and complications.

Post-Surgery Home Care

Our post-operative nursing care focuses on wound management, vital signs monitoring, and rehabilitation exercises to speed recovery and prevent complications.

24/7 Home Monitoring Benefits

Round-the-clock supervision, nurse alerts, and emergency response ensure safety, peace of mind, and immediate support when needed.
